About This Lesson

Candlestick charts are one of the most powerful tools in technical analysis. This comprehensive lesson will teach you how to read and interpret candlestick patterns to make better trading decisions.

You'll learn about the four key components of each candlestick: open, high, low, and close prices. We'll explore how the relationship between these prices creates different patterns that reveal market sentiment and potential price movements.

Through practical examples and real-world scenarios, you'll discover how professional traders use candlestick patterns to identify entry and exit points, predict trend reversals, and manage risk effectively.
